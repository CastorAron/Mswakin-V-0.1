Mswakini SAFARIS

Mswakini Safaris is privately owned safari outfitter with a dedicated team of professionals in the tourism industry as implementers of travel solutions in Tanzania. We provide high quality travel and tour services to both local and international clientele. While making travel plans, our main objective is to make travel safe, exciting and hassle free. Mswakini comes from Swahili word Mswaki (toothbrush in English) from Salvadora persica trees found in Mswakini village which is situated around 120km from Arusha and borders the Tarangire National Park

Mikumi National Park

Ruaha National Park

Rich of plethora of animals like buffalo, giraffe, kudu, sable, roan, lion, cheetah and leopard. It is a home to Tanzania’s biggest elephant population and stronghold for rare African wild dog.

Lake Natron

Lake Natron is a salt or soda lake not far from Lake Manyara in northern Tanzania. Lake Natron is quite shallow, less than three meters deep, and varies in width depending on its water level.

Lake Eyasi
